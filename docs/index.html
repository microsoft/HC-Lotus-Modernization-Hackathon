Why This Matters

The work is not just Lotus Notes app conversion. It is building a reusable modernization operating model that the Digital Transformation Branch (DTB) can apply across many migration scenarios, technologies, and legacy platforms.

By infusing AI-assisted engineering and modern development methodologies, we are enabling DTB developers to become self-sufficient modernization teams that reduce technical debt while accelerating enterprise delivery.

For Executives

How This PoC Enables DTB And Health Canada With AI

This proof of concept is designed to safely operationalize AI-assisted delivery in Digital Transformation Branch (DTB) and broader Health Canada development teams. The goal is to increase delivery throughput, shorten modernization cycles, and reduce legacy technical debt by redirecting engineering time from repetitive work to architecture, quality, and business-value features.

What We Are Enabling At Health Canada

  • Faster modernization across legacy workloads, including but not limited to Lotus Notes
  • A reusable operating model DTB can apply to multiple conversion types and technology stacks
  • Consistent engineering quality through AI-assisted coding plus mandatory human review
  • Reduction in tech debt by prioritizing refactoring and documentation, not only net-new features
  • Team capability uplift so developers can scale AI-assisted practices beyond this PoC

Evidence-based adoption, security-first execution, and repeatable delivery playbooks are the core design goals.
